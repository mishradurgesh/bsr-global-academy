Our Mission

Aiming High, Together

BSR Global Education Academy was founded with one clear purpose: to make expert, personalised education accessible to every motivated student on the planet. Our name is inspired by the archer — precise, disciplined, and always aiming true.

We are a team of passionate educators who have themselves studied and taught across international curricula. We know exactly what IB examiners are looking for, how to crack the SAT's hardest questions, and how to make a GCSE student love a subject they once feared.

From our base in India to students in Singapore, Australia, the UAE and beyond — every session we deliver is driven by the same conviction: a brilliant tutor, focused entirely on your child, changes everything.
